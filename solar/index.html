About Design Create Solar

Design Create Solar is a multidisciplinary club that focuses on creating solar based solutions by each of us, for all of us.


Our mission is to create exciting and effective solar based solutions to combat inequity at and beyond UCLA. While we are a goal oriented club, SOLAR was started by students for students. Our goal is to help new students apply their skills and to create a learning environment for new members to expand and grow in the directions that they want. We are currently pursuing four projects this fall: Solar Roof installation in the Jarash Refugee camp, Autonomous sun tracking solar chargers for campus, an outdoor hydroponic system with solar integration, and free solar powered transportation for the LA community. Along with our projects we will be helping students learn a plethora of skills through our eclectic and optional training program which we have been working hard on and plan to roll out this fall :)
