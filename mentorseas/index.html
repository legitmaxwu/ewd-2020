About MentorSEAS

The official mentorship organization for UCLA's Henry Samueli School of Engineering and Applied Sciences.


At the heart of the UCLA School of Engineering, MentorSEAS aims to guide young, aspiring engineers through UCLA's academic and societal environment. Incoming freshmen and transfer engineering majors are automatically paired with an undergraduate mentor, whose role is to help them in their transition into life at UCLA.
