About exploretech.la

exploretech.la is a student organization that hosts an annual outreach event aimed at encouraging and inspiring local high school students to pursue possibilities in STEM.


exploretech.la is a student organization that hosts an annual outreach event aimed at encouraging & inspiring local high school students to explore and pursue possibilities in computer science, engineering, and technology. During our event, our attendees are able to interact with representatives from various companies and organizations from both on and off the UCLA campus, participate in engaging workshops and activities, and learn from people in the industry about their experiences and career insights.

........

Any UCLA student is welcome to join exploretech.la! All majors are welcome, and no prior engineering experience is required. Applications for staff positions on our Operations and Content teams as well as for a new Webmaster position will be open during the beginning of Fall Quarter 2020. Our Operations team coordinates all our event logistics, our Content team crafts the workshops the students interact with, and the Webmaster will be responsible for maintaining and adding new features to our website.
