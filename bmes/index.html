About Biomedical Engineering Society

The Biomedical Engineering Society connects undergraduate students to academic and professional development resources, technical projects, community outreach opportunities, and social events to meet other undergraduates, graduates, and faculty. We welcome anyone who is interested in biomedical engineering!


BMES supports undergraduate students with navigating undergraduate life and post-graduate career options. We host academic and professional development events to help students with class planning, career advice, and internship opportunities. Additionally, we also have year-long technical projects and a workshop series for students of all experience levels to gain applicable skills in CAD, coding, circuitry, cell culture, and more. For students interested in volunteering, we outreach to local underserved communities with fun coding and circuitry curriculum. We also have mentorship families and club-wide socials to connect students with graduate students and faculty through events like Fall Barbeque, Holiday Party, Spring Banquet, Mentorship Cup Games, IM Sports, and more.
